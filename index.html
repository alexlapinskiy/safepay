Home

Every day, millions of payments are made on the Internet. Some of them are implemented with the help of phone attachments from card issuing banks, as well as integrated payment systems to the site. Every day, paying for products, appliances, home purchases, and services on the Internet, we risk our personal data. The most at risk is the information about the bank card. To provide a single system of secure payments, a versatile product with a range of capabilities, and most importantly with increased security, has been developed.

Description

Our project is designed to make secure transfers, payments, purchases and more on the Internet. To do this, you just need to download the app, add your most used cards, and you're done. You can also add your most used payment services.

Project Safepay

Safepay - a new application for your smartphone, with an increased level of security. Enhanced security is achieved by combining new data encryption methods, new ways to use keys. No more thinking about saving personal data, the app will do it instead of you. Remember only the pin code to open the application. More than hundreds of applications on the smartphone, Safepay is a centralized system for managing your transactions over the Internet.
